Introduction to Physics

Physics is the branch of science that studies matter, energy, and the fundamental forces of nature. It is the foundation for other sciences and has various applications in technology, medicine, and engineering.

Mechanics

Mechanics is the branch of physics dealing with the motion of objects and the forces that cause this motion.

Thermodynamics

Thermodynamics is the study of heat, energy, and the work they perform.

Electromagnetism

Electromagnetism is the study of electric and magnetic fields, and their interactions.

Quantum Physics

Quantum physics is the branch of physics that studies the behavior of matter and energy on the atomic and subatomic levels.
